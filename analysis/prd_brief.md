# PRD Brief

## Problem

Custom manufacturing buyers need confidence after checkout, especially when supplier handoffs, production steps, delays, partial shipments, or support exceptions make the order feel opaque.

## Proposed Product Bet

Create a post-order experience layer that combines line-item milestones, proactive delay alerts, and exception ownership so buyers can understand what changed, what happens next, and who owns resolution.

## Non Goals

- This artifact does not model real company performance.
- This artifact does not prescribe supplier-side compensation or pricing logic.
- This artifact does not replace qualitative buyer research.
